Hey, my name is Dylan, and I’m the lead developer. I wanted to make this game about game addiction because the problem is one that I deeply care about. I love playing video games, and making them is one of my favorite hobbies. Right now, the world has a hard time with communication. Less and less people are talking to others in real life. Of course, plenty of that comes from social media, political polarization, etc. But I think another reason is due to video game addiction. The best way to reach out to gamers: a video game.
