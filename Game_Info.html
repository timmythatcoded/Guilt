This is the Hud were the player will be able to get all of their info. The "time" icon tells the player how they have been in the undergrowth. The "Difficulty" icon is how hard your gameplay will be, difficulty will scale on how long you have been in the undergrowth, and how many souls you have collected. The "Souls" icon is the main scoring mechanic, it will also be used to factor the difficulty of the game. The higher the score the harder the game.



Once the Player has achieved a high enough difficulty, the undergrowth will send its strongest monster, The Leviathan, ] to come and chase the player. This monster is unkillable and will serve as a reminder that the player’s time in the undergrowth is slowly coming to an end.



You are not completely doomed however, when entering into the undergrowth apart from normal WASD movement. You are given a weapon that will allow you to shoot the monsters that lurk below. You are also able to dash away from any monster that gets to close, but will not be able to escape The Leviathan.
